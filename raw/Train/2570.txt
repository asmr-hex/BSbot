
Frame-Like
Action
and
Unfolded
Formulation
for
Massive
Higher-Spin
Fields
\\
Unfolded
equations
of
motion
for
symmetric
massive
bosonic
fields
of
any
spin
in
Minkowski
and
(A)dS
spaces
are
presented.
Manifestly
gauge
invariant
action
for
a
spin
$s
\ge
2$
massive
field
in
any
dimension
is
constructed
in
terms
of
gauge
invariant
curvatures.
