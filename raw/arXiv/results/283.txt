
Non-anticommutative
ABJ
Theory
\\
In
this
paper
we
will
discuss
non-anticommutative
deformations
of
the
harmonic
superspace.
We
will
analyse
the
non-anticommutative
deformation
of
the
superspace
that
break
the
supersymmetry
from
$\mathcal{N}
=3$
supersymmetry
to
$\mathcal{N}
=2$
supersymmetry.
We
will
then
study
the
ABJ
theory
in
this
non-anticommutative
superspace.
This
deformed
ABJ
theory
will
be
shown
to
posses
$\mathcal{N}
=5$
supersymmetry.
